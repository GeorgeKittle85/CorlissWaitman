Privacy Policy

1. Information Collection

Student Tools operates primarily as a client-side application. We may use cookies to remember your preferences and consent status, but we do not collect personal identifying information.

2. Cookies

We use minimal cookies to improve your experience. You can control cookie settings through your browser preferences. If you do not accept cookies, some features may be limited.

3. Data Storage

Any data you create in Student Tools (metronome settings, sheet music, etc.) is stored locally in your browser. We do not send this data to our servers.

4. Third-Party Services

Student Tools uses external libraries like VexFlow for rendering sheet music. These libraries may have their own privacy policies.

5. Your Privacy Rights

You have the right to request deletion of any stored preferences. Since we do not collect personal data, there is no personal information to delete.

6. Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at support@studenttools.com
